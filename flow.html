IT Administrator

FIRST USER · SYSTEM SETUP
You are the very first account on a clean install. Nothing happens until you configure the building blocks of attendance and invite the people who run the company.
1

Log in for the first time /login

Sign in with the seeded IT_ADMIN credentials. Because no one else exists yet, self-registration is disabled across the whole system — every other account must be invited.

2

Define a Geofence zone /dashboard/it-admin/geofence

Drop DevX Ltd HQ on the map and set its allowed radius (in metres). This becomes the virtual boundary every check-in is measured against.

💡 Why first: attendance can't be verified until the system knows where people are allowed to check in.
3

Generate the rotating QR /dashboard/it-admin/qrcodes

Activate a QR zone tied to the geofence. The token rotates on a timer so a screenshot can't be reused — layer two of the anti-proxy pipeline.

4

Invite the HR & admin team

Send invite-only registration links to the first HR Officer and other administrators. Each link is a time-limited token emailed to the recipient.

Enter email + role + department→System emails one-time link→ recipient registers
5

Watch everything via Audit & Security /it-admin/audit · /security

Every significant action — logins, approvals, record changes — is written to an append-only audit log you can review. The Security page surfaces active sessions and access controls.

🛡️ IT Admin done → hands the company over to HR →
👔

HR Officer

ONBOARDING · POLICY · OVERSIGHT
HR is the hub of the platform — you bring people in, set the rules they operate under, and keep the records honest. You have the widest menu in the system.
1

Invite employees & managers /dashboard/hr/invites

Enter each person's email, role and department. The system generates a one-time link and emails it. There is no public sign-up — this is how everyone joins.

2

Manage the workforce /dashboard/hr/users

Review every account, activate or deactivate users, and see who has enrolled their face. Fine-tune what each role can do under RBAC.

3

Set the rules /hr/policy · /hr/shifts

Configure leave types & balances, working shifts, and attendance policy. These drive late/absent detection and leave entitlements automatically.

4

Enroll & manage faces /dashboard/hr/face-profiles

Oversee biometric enrollment. Only an encrypted 128-d descriptor is kept — never a photo — so privacy is preserved by design.

5

Monitor live operations /hr/presence · /hr/attendance · /hr/leave

The Presence Board shows who is currently checked in. Review attendance records and act as the second approver on leave requests after the manager.

Employee applies→ Manager approves→ HR final approval
6

Sync, report & approve payroll /hr/sync · /hr/reports · /hr/payroll

Watch the Sync Engine keep attendance & leave consistent, pull org-wide reports, and give the HR sign-off on payroll runs before payslips go out.

👔 HR onboarded everyone → now the Employees can work →
🧑‍💻

Employee

DAILY USER
This is where the everyday magic happens. One place to clock in securely, manage leave, track tasks and read payslips — no paper, no email chains.
1

Accept invite & enroll face /register → /enroll-face

Open the emailed link, fill the registration form, then enroll your face through a guided wizard. The account activates only after enrollment.

2

Log in (password + face) /login → /face-verify

Enter your password, then pass a quick face check. Two factors, every login.

3

Check in — the 3-layer pipeline /dashboard/employee/check-in

The signature feature. Each layer must pass before a verified attendance record is created.

📍 Location inside geofence→ 🔲 Validate rotating QR→ 🙂 Face verify→ ✓ Checked in
💡 Fail any layer (wrong place, expired QR, no face match) and the attempt is logged & you retry — that's what kills proxy attendance.
4

Apply for leave /dashboard/employee/leave

Pick a type & dates, attach a document if needed, and submit. It routes to your manager, then HR. Approved leave auto-updates your attendance via the Sync Engine.

5

Work your allocated tasks /dashboard/employee/tasks

Every task a manager assigns to you appears on your board with its priority & due date. Tick off each sub-step, update your progress %, and the card moves To-Do → In Progress → Done. Add comments to ask questions without leaving the task.

See assigned task→ tick sub-steps→ update %→ manager sees it live
6

View attendance & payslips /employee/attendance · /payslips

Your monthly attendance calendar and downloadable payslips — with a clear breakdown of gross, PAYE, RSSB & net — all in one place. Dispute a payslip if something looks off.

7

Chat with the team /dashboard/chat

The built-in messenger keeps work talk inside STAFFSYNC. Join public channels open to everyone, your team channel, or start a direct message — created automatically on your first message.

📎 Files: image · video · doc · audio😀 Emoji reactions✓✓ Read receipts⏳ Ephemeral status
💡 Unread badges show what you've missed; you can only post to channels you've joined, and every message is written to the audit log.
🧑‍💻 Employee submits requests → the Manager reviews →
📋

Manager

TEAM LEAD · FIRST APPROVER
You see your team in real time and act as the first gate on leave and payroll. No more chasing email threads — everything waiting for you is in one queue.
1

Open your team dashboard /dashboard/manager

A live snapshot of your team — who's present, pending approvals, and task status — the moment you log in.

2

Watch presence /manager/team · /manager/presence

See who is checked in right now and where, instead of an out-of-date spreadsheet.

3

Approve leave (step 1 of 2) /dashboard/manager/approvals

Each request lands in your queue with the employee's reason. Approve or reject with a comment; approval forwards it to HR for the final sign-off.

Pending→ You approve→ HR finalizes→ Sync updates attendance
4

Allocate & manage tasks /dashboard/manager/tasks

Create a task — title, priority (Low → Urgent), due date and sub-steps — then assign it to a team member. The Kanban board moves it through its lifecycle and overdue work is flagged automatically.

📝 Create + sub-steps→ 👥 Assign→ 📈 Track %→ ✅ Done
TO-DOIN PROGRESSBLOCKEDIN REVIEWDONE
💡 Real-time: as employees tick sub-tasks & update progress, your board reflects it — and you can drop comments right on the task.
5

Sign off payroll (manager step) /dashboard/manager/payroll

When Finance submits a run, you review and approve the manager step before it moves to HR — a checkpoint for accuracy.

6

Pull team reports /dashboard/manager/reports

Attendance, leave and task summaries for your team, exportable for planning.

📋 Manager & HR approve the run → Finance finalizes pay →
💰

Finance Officer

PAYROLL · DEDUCTIONS · PAYSLIPS
You turn verified attendance into accurate pay. The system does the Rwanda-specific math automatically, so the monthly run is a few clicks instead of a few days of Excel.
1

Configure salaries /dashboard/finance/salaries

Set each employee's basic salary, allowances and overtime rate. This is the foundation every payroll run reads from.

2

Set up deductions /dashboard/finance/deductions

Manage the Rwanda statutory & custom deductions: PAYE, RSSB pension, RSSB medical, plus BRD/SACCO loans and union dues.

3

Create & process a payroll run /dashboard/finance/payroll

Start a run for the period. It pulls verified data, calculates gross, applies every deduction and produces net pay for all staff automatically.

run_create→ run_process→ run_submit→ approvals→ run_finalize
4

Route through approval

Submit the run — it passes through Manager → HR sign-off before you can finalize. Nothing is paid without the two-step check.

💡 This approval chain is the workflow-automation backbone: every step is logged & visible.
5

Finalize & send payslips /dashboard/finance/payslips

Once approved, finalize the run — payslips are generated and emailed to every employee in one step. Status flips to PAID.

6

Handle complaints /dashboard/finance/complaints

If an employee disputes a payslip, it arrives here for you to review and resolve — closing the loop on transparency.

💰 Pay sent → the cycle is complete & fully synchronized ✓
